### 1. 1. TERMS AND CONDITIONS APPLICABLE TO ALL EXCHANGES

EXCEPT FOR PERSONALIZED PRODUCTS, LIGHTERS, LINGERIE AND SWIMWEAR, ITEMS PURCHASED AT A DIRECTLY OPERATED SAINT LAURENT OR SAINT LAURENT RIVE DROITE STORE MAY BE EXCHANGED FOR AN IDENTICAL OR DIFFERENT ITEM ANYWHERE IN FRANCE AND ABROAD EXCLUSIVELY AT A STORE OF THE BRAND PURCHASED (I.E. RESPECTIVELY AT A SAINT LAURENT OR SAINT LAURENT RIVE DROITE STORE), IN ACCORDANCE WITH THE TERMS AND CONDITIONS SET FORTH IN SECTION 1.2, WITHIN THIRTY (30) DAYS OF THE PURCHASE DATE, SUBJECT TO THE FOLLOWING CONDITIONS:

- THE ITEM MUST NOT HAVE BEEN WORN, USED OR DAMAGED AND MUST BE RETURNED IN ITS ORIGINAL PACKAGING and,

SHOULD THE CUSTOMER EXCHANGE AN ITEM WITH A LOWER PRICED ITEM, SAINT LAURENT WILL ISSUE A CREDIT NOTE OR A REFUND, AS PER THE CUSTOMER'S REQUEST. SHOULD THE CUSTOMER EXCHANGE AN ITEM WITH A HIGHER PRICED ITEM, THE CUSTOMER SHALL PAY THE DIFFERENCE IN PRICE.
